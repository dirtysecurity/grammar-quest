## Games

### Grammar Quest
A Pokemon-themed grammar flashcard game for 3rd graders. Learn all 9 parts of speech through study cards, quiz battles, and sentence safari challenges.

**Features:**
- 3 game modes: Study Mode, Quiz Battle, Sentence Safari
- 9 grammar types: Noun, Verb, Adjective, Adverb, Pronoun, Conjunction, Preposition, Interjection, Article
- Pokemon sprites and themed content
- XP tracking and mastery system
- Grammardex collection screen
- Sound effects and animations

**Play:** [Grammar Quest](https://BoldSecurity.github.io/grammar-quest/grammar-quest/)
